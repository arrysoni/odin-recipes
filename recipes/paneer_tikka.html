Paneer Tikka Recipe

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and grilled or roasted to perfection. It's smoky, flavorful, and served with green chutney, making it a favorite in Indian cuisine. Traditionally cooked in a tandoor, it can also be made on a stovetop, oven, or air fryer.

Ingredients

For the Marinade

  • Paneer: 250g, cut into cubes
  • Thick yogurt (curd): ½ cup
  • Besan (gram flour): 2 tbsp (for binding)
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder: 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder: ¼ tsp
  • Coriander powder: 1 tsp
  • Garam masala: ½ tsp
  • Chaat masala: ½ tsp
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves): 1 tsp (crushed)
  • Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
  • Salt: to taste
  • Oil: 2 tbsp

For the Skewers

  • Bell peppers (capsicum): ½ cup, diced
  • Onion: 1, cut into square pieces
  • Tomato: 1, cut into cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Marinade
    • In a bowl, whisk yogurt, besan, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, garam masala, chaat masala, kasuri methi, lemon juice, salt, and oil.
    • Mix well until smooth and lump-free.
  2. Marinate the Paneer and Vegetables
    • Add paneer cubes, bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes to the marinade.
    • Gently coat everything well and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Oven Method
    • Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
    • Arrange the marinated paneer and veggies on skewers.
    • Place on a greased baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes, turning once in between.
  4. Serve
    • Sprinkle chaat masala on top for extra flavor.
    • Serve hot with green chutney and lemon wedges.
Enjoy your delicious homemade Paneer Tikka!
