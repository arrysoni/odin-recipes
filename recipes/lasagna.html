Vegetarian Lasagna Recipe

Description

Vegetarian lasagne is a delicious, layered pasta dish packed with wholesome vegetables, rich tomato sauce, and creamy cheeses. Unlike traditional meat lasagne, this version features a medley of sautéed veggies like bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms, and spinach, adding both flavor and nutrition. The layers of lasagne sheets, ricotta, mozzarella, and a hearty vegetable sauce create a comforting and satisfying meal. Baked to golden perfection, this dish is perfect for a cozy dinner and can be customized with different veggies and cheeses to suit your taste!

Ingredients

  • Lasagne sheets: 9 (pre-cooked or no-boil)
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Onion: 1, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Bell peppers: 1 cup, diced
  • Zucchini: 1, diced
  • Mushrooms: 1 cup, sliced (optional)
  • Spinach: 1 cup, chopped
  • Tomato puree: 2 cups
  • Italian seasoning: 1 tsp
  • Salt & pepper: to taste
  • Ricotta cheese (or paneer): 1 cup
  • Mozzarella cheese: 1.5 cups, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese: ¼ cup (optional)
  • Basil leaves: for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Veggie Sauce
    • Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
    • Add bell peppers, zucchini, and mushrooms; cook for 5 minutes.
    • Pour in tomato puree, add Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    • Stir in spinach and cook for another 2 minutes.
  2. Assemble the Lasagne
    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of veggie sauce.
    • Place lasagne sheets on top.
    • Spread a layer of ricotta cheese, followed by more veggie sauce and mozzarella.
    • Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  3. Bake
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes until golden brown.
  4. Serve
    • Let it cool for 5 minutes. Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm!
Enjoy your homemade veggie lasagne!
